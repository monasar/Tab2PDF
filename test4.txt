Durati-n Legend
---------------
W - wh-le; H - half; Q - quarter; E - 8th; s - 16th; T - 32nd; X - 64th; a - acciaccatura
+ - n-te tied t- previ-us; - - n-te d-tted; -- - n-te d-uble d-tted
Uncapitalized letters represent n-tes that are staccat- -1/2 durati-n-
Irregular gr-upings are n-tated ab-ve the durati-n line
Durati-n letters will always appear directly ab-ve the n-te/fret number it represents the
durati-n f-r-  Durati-n letters with n- fret number bel-w them represent rests-  Multi-
bar rests are n-tated in the f-rm W-n, where n is the number -f bars t- rest f-r-  L-w
mel-dy durati-ns appear bel-w the staff
